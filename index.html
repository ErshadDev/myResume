Mafia Game Project

In my Mafia game project, the focus is on distributing cards to players to determine their roles in the game. Players come together to engage in a collaborative gaming experience where they need to use strategy, deception, and deduction skills to uncover the identities of others and achieve their objectives within the Mafia game. By assigning unique roles to each player through the cards, I've created an immersive gameplay experience that challenges players to navigate the complexities of hidden identities and alliances. Through this distribution of role-based cards, my Mafia game project enhances the gameplay dynamics and strategic depth of the classic Mafia game, offering a new and compelling twist to the traditional game format.
